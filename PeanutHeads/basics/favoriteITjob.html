Web Designer

My favorite job would be a web designer, because it combines creativity and technology. The idea of making a website that is both beautiful and functional excites me. The atmosphere is dynamic and exciting with keeping up with the newest design trends and technologies. Also, collaborating with clients and other creatives to accomplish visions is a very fulfilling part of web design. The final product will help make users experiences enjoyable and meet business goals, which makes this profession very rewarding.
